About my works

I base myself on the idea that an architectural project involves various scales, from the most particular to the most global - from the choice of furniture to the design of the building - with everything being defined by a consistent set of formal and volumetric concerns. Everything the eye sees or the hand touches must be addressed and, in that sense, everything is architecture. With this in mind, I understand that a meticulous look and extreme attention to detail are very much necessary.
However, technique alone tells only part of the story. At the center of all my projects lies the client, from whom all inspiration is drawn to give the project an identity. It is from their ideas, needs and desires that I can create something truly meaningful. All of this is done with respect to the context and traditions, giving extreme importance to functionality, durability and user comfort.

Sobre os meus projetos

Me pauto na ideia de que um projeto de arquitetura envolve diversas escalas, desde as mais particulares até as mais globais - da escolha do mobiliário ao desenho do edifício - podendo tudo ser definido por um conjunto consistente de preocupações formais e volumétricas. Tudo que o olho vê ou a mão toca deve ser abordado e, nesse sentido, tudo é arquitetura. Para isso, entendo que é necessário um olhar minucioso e uma atenção extrema aos detalhes.
A técnica, entretanto, só conta uma parte da história. No centro de todos os meus projetos se encontra o cliente, do qual é extraída toda a inspiração para que o projeto apresente uma identidade. É a partir das suas ideias, necessidades e desejos que consigo criar algo verdadeiramente significativo. Isso tudo é feito com respeito ao contexto e às tradições, dando extrema importância à funcionalidade, à durabilidade e ao conforto do usuário.

About me

After going to business school for six months, I discovered that my true vocation is architecture and suspended my studies to enroll at FAU-Mackenzie, graduating as an architect and urban planner in 2023. I returned to my native country, England, to study at the renowned AA School on two separate occasions (in 2019 and 2022), where I had the opportunity to connect with individuals who inspired me to become an architect, such as Tatiana Bilbao and Virgil Abloh. I worked at internationally prestigious offices, such as Isay Weinfeld and Felipe Hess, where I had the opportunity to refine my architectural vision and understand the true exchange relationship between the architect and the client.
I am passionate about how computation and parametrization can contribute to a more comprehensive and efficient architectural production, from modeling to representation and commercialization. Hobby-wise, I enjoy reading and learning about architecture, programming, the financial market, and music. Additionally, I find great satisfaction in utilizing coding to streamline both my personal and professional life.
